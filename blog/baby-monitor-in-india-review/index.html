What is a Baby Monitor?

A baby monitor is a device which has two components, namely, the transmitter and the receiver. The transmitter is the part that is kept with the infant and depending on the type of monitor; it has a camera or a microphone or both. The second part of a baby monitor is called as the receiver which is kept with the parents that helps to look after their baby.

Safety measures when using a Baby Monitor

When handling a device around babies, it is necessary to take all necessary precautions to ensure his safety at all times. Make sure you are careful about the following safety tips when dealing with a baby monitor.

1- Placement of the baby monitor

Please make sure you are not placing the baby monitor inside the baby crib. It is also not advised to keep the device very close to the baby. It should be made sure that the baby monitor is kept a few feet away from the child.

2- Power plugs should be at a safe location

All power sources should be kept far away from the baby to keep him from any unfortunate happenings like short circuits. Power plugs should also be kept few feet away from the child to avoid suffocation.

3- Human protection can never be matched

The introduction of baby monitors was never meant as a replacement for human supervision. Even the best and latest technologies in a baby monitor cannot match the kind of security a parent provides to his child. Baby monitors are just advised to be used as an add-on to the safety measures for your baby.

4- Safety from Sudden Infant Death Syndrome (SIDS)

Although there are no confirmed causes of SIDS, a lot of baby monitors for detecting motion change are not SIDS safe. It should be kept in mind that baby monitors are not to be treated as a precaution for SIDS. However, it is advised that you should let your baby sleep on his back instead of his stomach or side.

 

Different types of Baby Monitors in India

There are many kinds of baby monitors that are available in the market. Depending on your needs and the features a particular type of baby monitor offers, you can choose the ideal type of baby monitor for your child. All these various types of baby monitors differ from each other by slight variations in their operations. The different types of baby monitors are: Baby Sound Monitors, Baby Video Monitors, Digital Monitors, Analog Baby Monitors, Wireless Network Monitors, and Baby Motion Monitors.

1- Baby Sound Monitors


The most popular baby monitor in India is Baby Sound Monitors that are available in both analog and digital modes. Like all monitors, this also has two major components; a transmitter and a receiver. The transmitter is kept in the babyâ€™s room and it catches any noise in the room and sends it over to the receiver which is kept with the parent to keep a check on their baby. There are some models that also come with more than one receiver so you can manage the baby monitor more efficiently.

2- Baby Video Monitors


Along with sound, baby video monitors also provide video monitoring of your baby to be extra careful for his safety while he is sleeping. In these types of baby monitors, there is a camera, a transmitter and a receiver. The camera and transmitter are placed with the baby in the babyâ€™s room to transmit image as well as sound to the receiver that is kept with you. Majority of the models in this category are very portable and easy to handle.

3- Digital Baby Monitors


Digital Monitors are the type of baby monitors that work on digital signals rather than analog frequencies. Because of this reason, other receivers cannot pick up the signals from digital monitors resulting in more clear signals on your receiver and eliminate all noises. This type of baby monitor are generally on the pricier end as compared to analog baby monitors as it provides enhanced sound quality and extra security.

4- Analogue Baby Monitors


Being operated on analog signals and frequency, analog baby monitors are not very expensive as compared to digital baby monitors. In analog monitors, other receivers or appliances operating on same frequency might interfere with the signals that the transmitter would be sending to your receiver. There is a high chance that you might sometime wake up to your neighborâ€™s baby crying as well due to this interference of signals.

5- Wireless Network Monitors


This type of baby monitors help you to connect different wireless devices that are already in your house which would mean that you can use your phones, computers, etc as the receiver of the baby monitors. The transmitter would send required information and notifications to these devices that are connected through wireless means.

6- Baby Motion Monitors


This is a more modern type of baby monitor which does not reflect the babyâ€™s change in movement. Neither does it transmit any sound from the babyâ€™s room. Instead, Baby Motion Monitors displays whether there was no movement in 20 seconds. They are generally wireless devices and the transmitter or the sensor is positioned below the childâ€™s mattress to monitor breathing patterns of the child after 20 seconds.

 

Factors to Consider When Buying Baby Monitor in India

Given the wide variety of different types of baby monitors, it can be a little confusing to choose just the right one only by types. We have specified certain features that you should look for in a baby monitor in order to ensure that your baby gets the best safety and you can stop worrying about him when he is away from you, especially when sleeping. In order to choose the best baby monitor for your baby, look for the following things in the device and make appropriate choices.

1- Uses either batteries, cords or cordless

Most of the baby monitors have two major parts, namely the transmitter and the receiver. The transmitter functions by non rechargeable batteries or an electrical cord for majority of the models. On the other hand, the receiver comes with rechargeable batteries or electrical cord. Many parents prefer a model with rechargeable batteries as well as an electrical cord as they wish to run the device at all times.

2- Area covered

Depending on the size of the babyâ€™s room, the distance between your room and his or the material of the walls, the area that is covered by baby monitors varies. It is recommended to think about all these factors while choosing a baby monitor. For example, an analog monitor gets weak when it has to travel through walls. Many models even have an indicator to display if you are out of range.

3- Portability

Some of the models are highly portable and can be moved around very easily whereas a lot of other products also require to be fixed or mounted on walls like the ones with cameras included. Few models also come with more than one receiver which makes the baby monitor more efficient to use.

4- Night Vision Technology

When considering buying a baby monitor that transmits both audio and video, it is important to ensure that night vision is also included in the video. So that if your baby sleeps in a dark room, you can properly see his video through the receiver and keep a check on him

5- Angles of the camera

When you have more than one camera installed, camera angles need to be kept in mind. A lot of different variations in the camera technology have been introduced in the category of baby monitors. All these cameras can be viewed in a single screen and are connected with the help of WiFi. The camera angles for these cameras can be manually controlled either manually or it can be automated in few modern products.

6- Analog or Digital Baby Monitors

The basic difference between analog and digital types of baby monitors for a parent is the noise related problems. When using an analog monitor, there can be interferences in frequencies and audio quality will not be very high. Noise can be produced. When digital monitors are used, the audio quality is high without any noise as there are no interference in the audio signal.
