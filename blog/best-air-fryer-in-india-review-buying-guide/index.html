What is an Air Fryer?

An air fryer is an appliance that air fries food by using very less or even no oil and gives you the taste of fried food without the damage of oil to your health. Working of an Air Fryer

The appliance, Air Fryer, is provided by many brands in India. Each model has some different unique feature. Air Fryers primarily can use the following technology or mechanisms to air fry food items to result in healthy and tasty dishes:

  1. Rapid Air Technology
  2. Exhaust System
  3. Cooling System

You can use an air fryer for frying, baking, toasting and even grilling food items. It consists of a heating element which provides heat which is evenly spread over the food with the help of a fan. Let us now understand the different technologies air fryers work upon.

1. Rapid Air Technology

This is the primary system on which Air Fryers usually work. Using this technology, the air fryer provides fast speed as compared to microwave ovens and frying pans. It is because of this technology the food items get quickly cooked and there is also an exhaust fan that produces hot air which helps to cook the food from all sides efficiently.

2. Exhaust System

Another important part of an air fryer is its exhaust system. The exhaust system helps to clean out smoke and steam that is present in the air fryer after food is cooked and the air is not released outside. So when your food is prepared, you shall only get the tasty smell of the food from the air fryer and not any kind of odour in your kitchen.

3. Cooling System

Besides an exhaust system, there is also a cooling system present in the air fryer which is highly useful for maintaining the temperature on the inside of the air fryer. The appliance works to make its parts cool by releasing air outside. Due to this very reason, it is recommended to serve the food as soon as it is prepared in the air fryer hot and tasty and not let it cool down.

 

Different types of Air Fryers to Buy in India

Various kinds of air fryers are available in the market. All the different types would have the same working as discussed earlier but their way of approaching the cooking process differs. Knowing about all the types of air fryers would help you decide which type suits you the best and would be perfect for your kitchen. The several types of Air Fryers are:-
– Round or Egg Shaped Air Fryer
– Air Fryer Ovens
– Halogen Air Fryers
– Paddle-type Air Fryers

1. Round or Egg Shaped Air Fryers

The most commonly found air fryers in the Indian household is the round or egg-shaped air fryers which are also known as Basket type air fryers. They are highly suitable for regular use in the Indian household and come with digital display and many other accessories so that you can easily operate the appliance.

2. Air Fryer Ovens

This next type of air fryers is recently introduced in India because of which you would not find a lot of products of this category. Instead of using pan or basket for air frying the food items, the air fryer ovens use horizontal racks just like an oven for cooking delicious items with less or no oil. It also gives you the advantage of cooking more than one dish at a time which makes it unique from the other types.

3. Halogen Air Fryers

As explained earlier majority of the air fryers have a heating technique like a conventional oven but the halogen air fryers use halogen bulbs for the same. The heat that these halogen bulbs emit is evenly spread and is used to cook the food item efficiently.

4. Paddle type Air Fryers

Doing justice to its name, paddle-type air fryers have a paddle which rotates to turn and stir the food when it is being cooked by the heat. This was the original type of air fryer when it was introduced.

 

Things to consider for buying the Best Air Fryer in India

Knowing all about air fryers also includes having information about the key factors which should play the most important role when deciding which air fryer to buy. We have listed some of the most important things or a few features that you need to be careful about while choosing the best air fryer for a healthy and tasty life ahead. Your requirements would be the main judge when you are choosing an air fryer; nonetheless, keeping a checklist in your mind based on these factors would eliminate all the confusion you would have about the appliance and what features to look for in the most ideal air fryer.

1- Quantity or Capacity

The capacity of the air fryer is a major factor to keep in mind when choosing an ideal air fryer. Depending on the size of your family you can invest in the appropriate air fryer. Generally, this capacity is specified in Liters and should be carefully noted when you are buying an air fryer.

2- Speed of Cooking

Air Fryers are known for cooking food items quickly as compared to pans and ovens. Along with reducing the consumption of oil, an air fryer is also capable of preparing the food in way less amount of time. Different models have a different speed to cook food and you should go for the most suitable one according to the features it provides.

3- Smoke Production

No matter what type of frying you are planning to do, smoke and steam is always produced. Many models come with a feature that screens the air that will come out of the air fryer which eliminated all the smoke and odour before the air is released outside.

4- Food Quality

There are many air fryers available that do not provide the best food quality all the time. It is necessary to invest in an air fryer that not only uses minimum oil but also results with great quality food. Otherwise, investing in a goof air fryer would not be fruitful at all. We recommend checking the quality of the appliance and also keeping in mind the quality of the food it can prepare.

5- Temperature Control

Many models come with a feature of controlling the temperature of the air fryer which shall allow you to cook food items at a certain temperature. To do the same, temperature control knobs are provided and some advanced models also provide buttons and even touch system to control the temperature of the appliance.
