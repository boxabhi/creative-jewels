What is an Instant Water heater?

As the name suggests, Instant water heaters are instant in a real sense and provide us with hot water supply as soon its switched on. Also known as the demand-type water heaters, these water heaters are tankless unlike storage water heaters and give a constant amount of hot water until switched off. The principle on which it works is just like an immersion rod that heats the water through an electric element but inside a container wherein the cold water travels through a pipe into the unit and gives us back the heated water through outlet pipe or hot water tap.

 

Pros & Cons of using an Instant Water heater

Pros

  • You get hot water instantly as soon as you switch the Geyser
  • It has lower operating and energy cost
  • Have a life expectancy of longer than 20 years
  • The parts are easily replaceable and available
  • Its energy-efficient than storage water heaters
  • Suitable for bachelors and small-sized family
  • Saves a lot of space
  • Both gas geyser & electric eater heater options are available
  • Water supply is unlimited

Cons

  • Inconsistent water temperatures
  • Installation is costly
  • Though unlimited the flow of water supply is slower
  • It cannot be used for diverse purposes at the same time

 

Things to consider while buying an Instant water Geyser

1. Type

It’s essential to choose the type of instant water heater that comes with two options. The gas instant geysers and the electric instant geyser. The main factor in differentiating in buying gas and electric water heaters is their efficiency and safety.

2. Capacity-

Selecting the right size and how much capacity of water the water heater gives at a time should depend upon the need of your family. To meet a family demand is an essential factor to consider before buying the instant water heater as they are tank-less and do not store water to buffer any shortage.

3. Energy Consumption

Saving electricity is the main benefit of an instant water heater, so it’s essential to pay close attention while choosing the one as per your need.

4. Safety features

Safety is a crucial factor if you are considering to buy a gas instant water heater. There are chances of leakage with a gas water heater, so some brands offer safety features like deducting leakage and child lock and auto cutoff in electrical instant geyser to avoid any accidents.

5. Installation space

Instant water heaters are mostly compact and can be installed in smaller areas of space; also, it’s crucial to establish a gas instant geyser at such a height where it can be switched off quickly to avoid any overheating.
