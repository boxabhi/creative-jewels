About Creative Jewels

Whether wishing a birthday, wedding-anniversary, house-warming ceremony, newborn’s arrival, health or want to show gratitude by giving a personalized return gift, what better way than to say it with the sweetness of chocolates, cookies, donuts and cakes! We offer you quality products, free from chemical preservatives, handmade, especially for you! We make chocolates and cookies in small batches and each batch is quality tested for freshness, taste, flavor, consistency and perfection. Each product is made with love, with hand-picked and fresh ingredients, customized according to your personal liking. We bring to you extinguished, unique and creative flavors to excite your taste buds, an option you would generally not get in branded and factory-packaged products. With ‘beaming smiles’ and ‘happy faces’ as our motto, we promise to give your loved ones some tasty memories to cherish! So, which delicious delight are you planning to gift to your beloved ones?
